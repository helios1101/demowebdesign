Executive Body Meet
The Executive Body Meeting of the Association has been fixed on June 06th 2014 from 10:00 Hrs. to 18:00 Hrs. at Bodhgaya. The venue of the meeting will be commenced shortly. The agenda of the Executive Body Meeting are as under:-
  • Welcome Address by the President
  • Reading of Minutes of last General Body Meeting by General Secretary
  • Action Taken Report of General Secretary
  • Issues relating to Cadre Review
  • Discussion on AGT-2014
  • Discussion on Welfare Fund for Inspectors
  • Any other issue with permission of the Chair.
